About Me

Hi! I’m Alexander, currently a junior college student planning on transferring to a university in 2023. I am passionate about machine learning and data science, specifically at the intersection of human-computer interaction and data innovation. I have worked on a team with IBM to promote digital access and literacy to underserved communities globally. I have been mentored by engineers at Intel in Folsom, CA and I am the current Vice President of the ACM Los Rios Chapter. Outside of school, I am a fan of mindsetting with Vipassana or insight-based daily meditation and self-improvement through calisthenics, hatha and vinyasa-flow yoga, and better living. Currently, I am working on publishing a series of short stories about love, adventure, and life. I've also formed a learning community with friends from all over the world persuing knowledge and practice. With my team, I've worked on learning French, German, Italian, and Russian.
